FAQ

Good to know

What is BOOSTER?

BOOSTER is a community token on BASE built around live booster-pack openings. We buy sealed packs, rip them on stream, and the pulls go to the community. $BOOST is the token that powers it.

Which chain is it on?

BASE — the Ethereum L2 by Coinbase. $BOOST is a standard ERC-20, so it works with any BASE-compatible wallet (Coinbase Wallet, MetaMask, Rabby…).

Are the packs real?

Yes. Every pack is genuine and sealed, opened live on camera. No edits, no swaps — what you see pulled is what you get.

How do I get my cards?

Winners choose: have the physical cards shipped worldwide, or take an instant payout in $BOOST at market value.

How do I buy $BOOST?

Bridge ETH to BASE, open Aerodrome or Uniswap, and swap ETH for $BOOST using the official contract address (published at launch — always verify it here).

Is this gambling?

Opening packs carries an element of chance and BOOSTER is high-risk entertainment, not an investment. Know your local laws, only spend what you can afford to lose, and 18+ only.
